Bootstrap 5 Utility Classes Cheat Sheet

Spacing

-   m-0 to m-5: margin on all sides.
-   mt-, mb-, ms-, me-: top, bottom, start(left), end(right).
-   mx-*: horizontal margin.
-   my-*: vertical margin.
-   p-0 to p-5: padding on all sides.
-   pt-, pb-, ps-, pe-, px-, py-: directional padding.

Text

-   text-start: left align.
-   text-center: center.
-   text-end: right align.
-   text-primary, text-success, text-danger, text-warning, text-info,
    text-dark, text-light, text-white.
-   fw-bold, fw-semibold, fw-normal, fst-italic.
-   fs-1 to fs-6: font size.
-   text-decoration-none.
-   text-uppercase, text-lowercase, text-capitalize.

Display

-   d-none, d-block, d-inline, d-inline-block.
-   d-flex: enable flexbox.
-   d-grid: enable CSS Grid.

Flexbox

-   flex-row, flex-column.
-   justify-content-start, center, end, between, around, evenly.
-   align-items-start, center, end, stretch.
-   align-self-start, center, end.
-   flex-wrap, flex-nowrap.
-   gap-1 to gap-5.

Width & Height

-   w-25, w-50, w-75, w-100, w-auto.
-   h-25, h-50, h-75, h-100.
-   vh-100: full viewport height.

Grid

-   container, container-fluid.
-   row.
-   col, col-6, col-md-4, col-lg-3.
-   g-0 to g-5: gutter spacing.

Forms

-   form-control: text input.
-   form-label: label styling.
-   form-select: dropdown.
-   form-check: checkbox/radio wrapper.
-   form-check-input.
-   form-check-label.
-   input-group: prepend/append text or buttons.
-   is-valid, is-invalid: validation styles.

Buttons

-   btn.
-   btn-primary, secondary, success, danger, warning, info, light, dark.
-   btn-outline-*.
-   btn-sm, btn-lg.
-   disabled.

Background & Borders

-   bg-primary, secondary, success, danger, warning, info, light, dark,
    white.
-   border, border-0.
-   border-primary, border-danger, etc.
-   rounded, rounded-0, rounded-1..5, rounded-circle, rounded-pill.
-   shadow, shadow-sm, shadow-lg.

Position

-   position-relative, position-absolute.
-   fixed-top, fixed-bottom.
-   sticky-top.
-   top-0, bottom-0, start-0, end-0.
-   translate-middle.

Overflow & Visibility

-   overflow-auto, overflow-hidden, overflow-scroll.
-   visible, invisible.

Responsive Prefixes

Use with most utilities: - sm (>=576px) - md (>=768px) - lg (>=992px) -
xl (>=1200px) - xxl (>=1400px)

Examples: - text-md-center - d-lg-flex - col-md-6 - mt-lg-5
